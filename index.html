As you may have noticed, my name is Kaci Witter. I'm working on getting my bachelor's degree in Digital Innovations at Arkansas State University. I've always loved art in many forms. When making something, I try my best to think outside the box. It's so easy to recreate the same things that other designers have done time and time again. Of course, that is a major part of design, but those that stand out add their own unique touch to the ideas of others. In my design career, I will constantly strive to add my own twist to the design world. I try my best not to stress and instead have fun with it. I've enjoyed learning so far and I'm excited to see what's ahead.
